About BioPedia

Free knowledge, for every learner, everywhere.

Our Mission

  • BioPedia exists to make life sciences education free, clear, and accessible to everyone — regardless of location, background, or financial means.
  • Whether you're a school student, a college researcher, or simply a curious mind, BioPedia aims to be your go-to reference for biology.
  • We cover every major branch of life sciences, from foundational basics to research-level concepts.

What You'll Find Here

  • Clear, point-wise theory covering Biochemistry, Molecular Biology & Genetics, Cell Biology & Signaling, Botany, Zoology, Ecology, Evolution, Developmental Biology, and Biotechnology.
  • Diagrams and visuals to make complex concepts easier to understand.
  • A clean, distraction-free reading experience with light and dark mode.
  • Constantly growing content — new topics and subjects are added regularly.

Why It's Free

  • We believe quality science education shouldn't be locked behind paywalls.
  • BioPedia will always remain free to read, for students and lifelong learners alike.
  • The site is supported through minimal, unobtrusive advertising — never through fees to our readers.

Get in Touch

  • Found an error, or have a suggestion? We'd love to hear from you.
  • BioPedia is a growing project, and reader feedback directly shapes what we build next.
